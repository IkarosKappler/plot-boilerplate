
Simple:
-------

\begin{align*}
A,B \epsilon \mathbb{N} \\
Lissajous_A_,_B: \mathbb{R} \mapsto \mathbb{R}\times\mathbb{R} \\
\phi &\mapsto ( sin(A*\phi), sin(B*\phi) )  \\
\end{align*}


Extended:
---------

\begin{align*}
A,B \epsilon \mathbb{N} \\
phaseA, phaseB \epsilon \mathbb{R} \\
Lissajous_A_,_B: \mathbb{R} \mapsto \mathbb{R}\times\mathbb{R} \\
\phi &\mapsto ( sin(phaseA + A*\phi), sin(phaseB + B*\phi) )  \\
\end{align*}
